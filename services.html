Zero Human Handling
Service 01

Fully Automated Robotic Retrieval

Our robotic retrieval arm is the heart of the Secure Shield Vault system. When you authenticate and request your box, the system locates your specific deposit box within our high-density vault hall and retrieves it with millimetre precision — entirely without human involvement.

This means no staff member, no security guard, and no third party ever has access to your box. The robot brings it to you. You take what you need. The robot returns it. Clean. Private. Absolute.

  • Fully automated retrieval — no staff involvement at any stage
  • High-density vault hall maximises security and capacity
  • Precise, repeatable mechanical handling prevents damage
  • Every retrieval event is logged with timestamp and biometric record
  • System operates continuously — no downtime, no delays
Identity-Only Access
Service 02

Biometric Identity Lock

Traditional vaults use keys and PIN codes — both of which can be copied, stolen, or shared. Secure Shield Vault uses your unique biological signature as the sole authentication credential. There is no workaround.

Our multi-factor biometric system combines fingerprint scanning and facial recognition at every access point. The system cross-references your registered identity in real time before any retrieval command is issued to the robotic system.

  • Fingerprint authentication at entry and retrieval
  • Facial recognition secondary verification
  • No keys, cards, or PINs — nothing to lose or steal
  • Registered biometrics encrypted and stored securely on-site
  • Failed authentication triggers immediate alert protocol
24/7 RVM Surveillance
Service 03

Robotics Vision Monitoring

Our RVM (Robotics Vision Monitoring) CCTV system goes beyond standard surveillance. Every camera in our facility is networked into an intelligent monitoring system that combines high-definition video capture with automated anomaly detection.

Coverage is total — vault hall, access corridors, reception, exterior entry, and all mechanical systems. Footage is encrypted and retained in cloud storage, accessible by authorised security personnel only.

  • Full-facility coverage with zero blind spots
  • High-definition capture around the clock, 365 days a year
  • Automated anomaly detection triggers instant alerts
  • Encrypted cloud footage retention for full audit trail
  • All CCTV data is kept strictly confidential and secure
Fully Insured Items
Service 04

Premium Insurance Cover

Physical security alone is not enough. That is why every item stored at Secure Shield Vault carries comprehensive insurance cover, underwritten by leading insurers with experience in high-value asset protection.

From the moment your valuables enter our facility, they are covered. Jewellery, documents, cash, precious metals, digital media — all protected by a policy that reflects the true value of what you are storing.

  • All stored items automatically covered from date of deposit
  • Partnership with premium underwriters specialising in valuables
  • Claims process is straightforward and client-focused
  • Insurance cover details provided at time of registration
  • Cover scales with box size and declared item value

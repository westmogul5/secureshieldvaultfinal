2026

The Beginning

A Nairobi-First Revolution in Secure Storage

For decades, securing precious valuables in Kenya meant trusting institutions with limited transparency, outdated technology, and uncertain insurance. Secure Shield Vault was founded to change that reality — completely.

Drawing on global best practices from the world's most advanced vault facilities in Zurich, Singapore, and Dubai, our founders brought fully automated, robotics-driven safe deposit technology to Nairobi for the first time.

"Security should never be a compromise. It should be absolute."

Located at Malik Heights on Ngong Road, our facility is engineered from the ground up with military-grade steel, biometric access control, and RVM CCTV surveillance operating every second of every day.

Our Journey

How We Built the Future

From concept to Kenya's most advanced vault — the story of Secure Shield Vault.

2023

The Vision

Our founders identify a critical gap in Kenya's secure storage market. Research begins into global robotic vault systems, biometric technology, and insurance frameworks. Feasibility studies conducted across Nairobi's business districts.

2024

Engineering & Design

Partnerships formed with world-leading vault robotics engineers. The Malik Heights facility is selected and begins transformation into East Africa's most secure storage environment. Military-grade vault construction commences.

2025

Technology Integration

Full robotic retrieval systems installed and tested. Biometric access control infrastructure deployed. RVM CCTV surveillance network activated. Premium insurance partnerships secured for all stored items.

2026

Kenya's first fully automated, insured safe deposit vault facility opens at Malik Heights, Ngong Road. Three box sizes — Medium, Large, and X-Large — are made available to individuals, families, and businesses across East Africa.

The Technology

What Makes Us Different

Four pillars of technology combine to deliver an unmatched level of protection for your most valuable assets.

Robotic Automation

Our proprietary robotic retrieval arm locates, retrieves, and returns your safe deposit box with zero human intervention. No staff member ever handles your box — only the machine, and only under your biometric authorisation.

Biometric Identity

Multi-factor biometric authentication — including fingerprint and facial recognition — ensures that only the registered box holder can ever initiate a retrieval. No key cards. No PINs. No workarounds.

RVM CCTV Surveillance

Advanced Robotics Vision Monitoring (RVM) CCTV covers every centimetre of our facility, including all access corridors, the vault hall, and external entry points — 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, with encrypted cloud footage retention.

Premium Insurance

Every item stored at Secure Shield Vault is backed by comprehensive insurance cover. Our partnership with leading underwriters means your valuables are protected beyond the physical vault — from the moment they enter our facility.
